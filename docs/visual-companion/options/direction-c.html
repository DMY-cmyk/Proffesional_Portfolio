The Effect of Sustainability Report Disclosure on Firm Value in the Manufacturing Sector

This study examines the relationship between sustainability disclosure quality and firm value among publicly listed Indonesian manufacturers, drawing on GRI Standards and ESG indicators. Using panel data regression across a 5-year window, the research investigates whether investors reward transparency on environmental and social metrics — and under what conditions.
